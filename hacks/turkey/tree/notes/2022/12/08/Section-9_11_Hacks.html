1) The starting amount of money is $10 2) The variable, spendMoney, is set to True 3) While spendMoney remains true, $1 is deducted from the money variable 4) Once the variable, money, reaches a value of 0, spendMoney is set to False and the string, "You're out of money!" is printed.

money = 10
spendMoney = True
while(spendMoney == True):
    money -= 1
    if money == 0:
        spendMoney == False
        print("You're out of money!")
